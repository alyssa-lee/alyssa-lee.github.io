I'm a current junior in the Computational Biology Department at CMU's School of Computer Science. My research interests involve using machine learning to analyze biological datasets. I currently work with Ph.D. student [Caleb Ellington](https://cnellington.github.io/) on contextualized machine learning. We are a part of the [SAILING Lab](https://sailing-lab.github.io/), led by [Eric P. Xing](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~epxing/). 

In addition to computational biology research, I have experience in web development. See my CV for more information.

Outside of coding, I play the oboe in CMU's All University Orchestra. I also enjoy baking and reading books.
