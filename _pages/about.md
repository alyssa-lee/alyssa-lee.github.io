I'm a current senior at CMU's School of Computer Science. I've worked on modeling biological systems with machine learning, data analysis in the pharmaceutical industry, and software development. I'll graduate in May 2024.

I'm excited to share that I'll be spending the spring 2024 semester at the [Williams-Mystic Coastal & Ocean Studies Program](https://mystic.williams.edu/), where I'll take interdisciplinary courses in science, history, literature, and policy, conduct independent research, and visit a few different coastal regions!

Outside of coding, I play the oboe in CMU's All University Orchestra and help run the undergraduate Computational Biology Society. I also enjoy baking and reading books.
