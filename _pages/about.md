I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with my B.S. in Computational Biology in May 2024! In August, I'll start my new job as a Bioinformatics Analyst at the [University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine, Department of Pediatrics](https://www.pediatrics.pitt.edu/research/cores-and-research-support/bioinformatics-core). In the past, I've worked on modeling biological systems with machine learning, analyzing data from preclinical toxicology studies, and developing software. I'm interested in a wide variety of topics, and sometimes I write about them on this blog.

I spent my final semester at the [Williams-Mystic Coastal & Ocean Studies Program](https://mystic.williams.edu/), where I studied science, history, literature, and policy, conducted independent research, and visited a few different coastal regions! I wrote about my experiences on this blog.

At Carnegie Mellon, I was co-president of the undergraduate Computational Biology Society and played oboe in the All University Orchestra. I also enjoy baking and reading books.
