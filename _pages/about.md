I'm a computational biology major graduating from the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in May 2024! I've worked on modeling biological systems with machine learning, analyzing data from preclinical toxicology studies, and developing software applications. I'm interested in a wide variety of topics related to biology, and sometimes I write about them on this blog.

I'm excited to share that I'm spending the spring 2024 semester at the [Williams-Mystic Coastal & Ocean Studies Program](https://mystic.williams.edu/), where I'll take interdisciplinary courses in science, history, literature, and policy, conduct independent research, and visit a few different coastal regions!

At Carnegie Mellon, I was co-president of the undergraduate Computational Biology Society and played oboe in the All University Orchestra. I also enjoy baking and reading books.
