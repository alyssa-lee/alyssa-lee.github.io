I'm a current senior majoring in Computational Biology at Carnegie Mellon's School of Computer Science. I've worked on data analysis in the pharmaceutical industry, modeling biological systems with machine learning, and agile software development. I'm looking for my next opportunity in the form of a full-time job after I graduate in May 2024.
